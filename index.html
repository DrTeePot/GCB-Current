Dance into Summer

The repertoire for our next concert will emphasize distinctive dances and musical styles from all around the world. The pieces selected for this concert represent a variety of cultures and are carefully orchestrated to represent the real folk characters of different traditions. You will enjoy the harmonies of the Slavonic Dances by Dvorak, electrifying Jan Van der Roost's Gypsy song tempos and Klezmer or Arabesque melodies that will make this concert a unique experience.
Funds from this event will go towards supporting the Guelph Concert Band.

Sunday May 31, 2015; 3PM

Harcourt Memorial Church (87 Dean Avenue, Guelph, ON)
Tickets:
Adults: $15 | Seniors and University Students: $10 | eyeGO and Children: $5
