From Havana to Guelph

Enjoy the sizzling sounds of Havana with the Guelph Concert Band and featured special guest, Cuban legend and jazz master, Bobby Carcasses. Mr. Carcasses is famous for his contribution to Latin jazz and is a founding organizer of the renowned Jazz Plaza Festival in Cuba. As performer, Bobby can be found playing trumpet, bass, congas, drums, piano and scat singing in venues all over the world. Also featured are the incredible Russian ballet dancer Ekaterina Tikanova, the enchanting songress Mary DuQWuesnay and the energizing Saint-John’s Kilmarnock School Jazz Band.

Feb. 22, 2015 at 3:00 PM
River Run Centre
Tickets $24 adults, $15 seniors and students, $5 eyeGO and children
