Rehearsals

We rehearse every Sunday evening at 7pm at the Guelph Youth Music Centre, 75 Cardigan St (see map). If you would like to join us for a rehearsal please contact us at info@guelphconcertband.ca so we can be prepared to welcome you.

New players are always welcome in any section. We are especially looking for clarinet, french horn, bassoon, string bass, trombone and percussion players at the moment.
